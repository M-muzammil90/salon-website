Nail Care Tips

Keep Nails Hydrated

Apply cuticle oil daily and moisturize hands regularly to prevent dry, brittle nails.

Use Protective Base Coat

Always apply a base coat before polish to prevent staining and help manicures last longer.

Proper Nail Filing

File nails in one direction rather than sawing back and forth to prevent splitting and damage.
